ABOUT ME

I am a collage artist from Los Angeles, CA with interests in photography, film, and music production. I graduated in December 2019 at California State University, Northridge with a B.A. in Cinema and Television Arts - Multimedia Production. I draw inspiration from vintage magazines to create my collages and admire every detail in the photos I select. The art of manipulating photos has peaked my interest since I was a young teen.
